Part: Zipper (Industrial Grade)
- Standard tooth alignment tolerance: 0.1mm.
- Common defects: Misalignment, cooling cracks, surface oxidation.
- Maintenance: Lubricate every 5,000 cycles using silicone-based oil.

Part: Glass Bottle (Food Grade)
- Crack tolerance: 0.0mm (Zero tolerance).
- Surface bubble size limit: < 0.5mm.
- Reject criteria: Any vertical cracks longer than 1mm on the neck area.

Part: Metal Bolt (Grade 8.8)
- Torque specification: 15-20 Nm.
- Thread pitch: 1.25mm.
- Defect note: Burrs on the thread area must be removed before installation.
